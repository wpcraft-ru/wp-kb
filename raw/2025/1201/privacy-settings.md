## Find the privacy settings
To access your site’s privacy options:
1. Visit your site’s [dashboard](https://wordpress.com/home/).
2. Navigate to *Settings →* *Reading*.
1. Scroll to the **Site Visibility** section.
2. Choose from three privacy options (or, [launch your site](https://wordpress.com/support/privacy-settings/launch-your-website/)):
* [Coming Soon](https://wordpress.com/support/privacy-settings/display-a-coming-soon-page/): Your site is hidden from visitors while you’re working on it. Use this to temporarily unpublish your website. You can share a preview link with select people.
* [Public](https://wordpress.com/support/privacy-settings/make-your-website-public/): Your site is visible to everyone.
* [Private](https://wordpress.com/support/privacy-settings/make-your-website-private/): Your site is only visible to you and any users you approve. Use this to unpublish your website and restrict access to approved users only.
3. Click the **“Save Changes”** button at the bottom of the page.
