# The best WordPress hosting services of 2026: Expert tested and reviewed
> Source: https://www.zdnet.com/article/best-wordpress-hosting/

ZDNET has extensively tested and reviewed many WordPress hosting providers for years.

## Best WordPress hosting providers of 2026

### Hostinger — Best Overall
Hostinger is an all-rounder with affordable pricing tiers. Plans begin at $2/mo with three months free.
Features: e-commerce support, one-click WordPress installation, automatic updates, management tools, vulnerability scanner, DDoS protection, 24/7 support. AI tools and Kodee AI agent available.

### WP Engine — Best for SMBs
Supports 5M+ websites. Key features: scalability and "transferable installs." ISO 27001:2022 certified. Plans from $30/mo.
Features: Design/build tools, 1-click staging, rollback, strong security, headless WordPress, 24/7 support.

### ScalaHosting — Best Short-Term Affordability
Entry plan at $2.95/mo. Shared and dedicated WordPress hosting, unlimited websites, free SSL, daily backups, staging, anti-malware, free migration, one-click installer.
Storage: 10GB to 50GB+. Trustpilot: 5/5.

### WordPress.com — Best for E-Commerce
E-commerce plans: 50GB storage, free domain 1yr, premium themes, plugin support, WooCommerce. From $45/mo (annual).

### Pagely — Enterprise-Grade
GoDaddy brand. Clients: Disney, VMware, Cisco. Plans from $99-499/mo. AWS infrastructure. 24/7 support. Trustpilot: 4.5/5.

- Media/bandwidth requirements
- Security (DDoS protection)
- Free trials availability
- Support channels
