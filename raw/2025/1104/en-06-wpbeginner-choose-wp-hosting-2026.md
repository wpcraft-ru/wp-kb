## WordPress Hosting Requirements
- PHP 7.4+
- MySQL 5.7+ or MariaDB 10.4+
- HTTPS support (SSL certificate)

## Types of Hosting
- **Free**: Unreliable, forced ads, not recommended
- **Shared**: Most popular for beginners, affordable
- **VPS**: More control, medium businesses
- **Dedicated**: Maximum performance, enterprise
- **Managed**: Hands-off, provider handles everything

## Testing Methodology
Tools: K6 (GrafanaLabs) for stress testing, Pingdom for speed, Bitcatcha for server response, Uptime Robot for monitoring.
