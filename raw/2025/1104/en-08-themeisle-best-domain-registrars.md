## Top 3 Quick Recommendations

**Best overall:** Namecheap — excellent promos, fair renewals, scalable hosting, AI website builder.

**Best long-term pricing:** Cloudflare — sells domains at cost, no markup. No hosting available.

**Best with free email:** IONOS (2GB mailbox) or Dynadot (1GB mailbox).

## Detailed Reviews

### GoDaddy (11.53% market share)
- ~550 TLDs, free WHOIS privacy
- Web hosting: shared, WordPress, VPS, reseller
- Domain appraisal, auctions, brokerage
- AI tool: GoDaddy Airo™
- Support: phone (30+ countries), chat, WhatsApp
- Watch for high renewal prices ($21.99/yr for .com)

### Namecheap (3.06% market share)
- ~550 TLDs, free WHOIS privacy
- Web hosting: shared, WordPress, VPS, dedicated
- Free: domain email forwarding, DNS, CDN, Stencil design tool
- VPN from $0.99/mo
- Support: ticket, live chat 24/7

### Squarespace Domains (1.17%)
- ~630 TLDs, free WHOIS privacy
- Acquired Google Domains in 2023
- Free: SSL, premium DNS, coming soon page
- Web hosting only via Squarespace builder

## Selection Criteria
- Market share (user validation)
- Real user experiences (Reddit)
- Professional judgement
