# 6 Best Domain Name Registrars of 2025 (Compared)
> Source: https://www.elegantthemes.com/blog/wordpress/best-domain-name-registrars

A domain name registrar is an ICANN-accredited business that sells and registers domains.

## Best Domain Name Registrars

### 1. Namecheap
- From $8.98/yr (.com)
- TLDs: .co, .org, .net, .com, .dev, .app, country-specific
- FreeDNS hosting + PremiumDNS (DDoS protection)
- Handshake blockchain TLDs (.oh, .creator)
- Best for: freedom and control over domain

### 2. SiteGround
- From $17.99/yr (.com)
- Broad selection of extensions
- Bundle with hosting: StartUp, GrowBig, GoGeek
- Free SSL, CDN, Divi + WordPress pre-installed
- Best for: managing domain + hosting in one place

### 3. GoDaddy
- From $11.99/yr (.com)
- 500+ domain extensions
- Domain broker service for taken domains
- Free privacy protection
- Full/Ultimate Domain Protection plans
- Best for: straightforward setup, privacy, acquiring taken domains

### 4. Name.com
- From $1.99 (on sale)
- Bulk domain search
- Expiring and premium domains
- Free: URL forwarding, email forwarding, URL shortener
- WHOIS privacy available
- Best for: cheap domains, keyword domains

### 5. Bluehost
- From $12.99/yr (.com)
- Popular TLDs + geo-location names
- Domain forwarding, auto-renewals
- Microsoft 365 integration
- 24/7 domain support
- Best for: easy management, expert support

### 6. Spaceship
- From $8.48/yr (.com)
- Fastest-growing registrar (launched 2023)
- Unbox™ feature for seamless product integration
- Free WHOIS privacy forever
- Best for: all-in-one website solution

## Key Considerations
- ICANN accreditation
- TLD variety
- DNS management capabilities
- Additional services (hosting, email, SSL)
- Privacy protection
- Renewal pricing transparency
