# Storefront
Last updated on January 23rd, 2025 by Syed Balkhi
[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https://www.isitwp.com/wordpress-themes/storefront/&display=popup&ref=plugin&src=share_button)[Tweet](https://twitter.com/share?url=https://www.isitwp.com/wordpress-themes/storefront/&text=Storefront)[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/shareArticle?mini=true&url=https://www.isitwp.com/wordpress-themes/storefront/)[Pin](https://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=https://www.isitwp.com/wordpress-themes/storefront/&media=https://www.isitwp.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/storefront-theme-review-e1474376635417.png&description=Storefront)Shares0
Storefront is the official WooCommerce theme for WordPress, available for free from the WordPress.org directory. It’s a lightweight, basic theme that’s an excellent base for creating your online shop. Read our Storefront review to find out if you should run your eCommerce site on this theme.
## About Storefront
Storefront was built by the developers behind [WooCommerce](https://www.isitwp.com/wordpress-plugins/woocommerce/), the most popular eCommerce plugin for WordPress. Using WooCommerce and the Storefront theme, you can quickly and easily [set up an online shop](https://www.isitwp.com/how-to-create-an-online-store-step-by-step/) to sell your products or services.
Storefront serves as a lightweight, basic theme to quickly create a functional online store. Though it includes basic customization options, if you want a more unique style you can invest in one of the premium [child themes](http://www.wpbeginner.com/beginners-guide/wordpress-child-theme-pros-cons/).
**Also check:** Best [eCommerce Plugins for Online Stores](https://www.isitwp.com/best-wordpress-ecommerce-plugins-compared/).
## Features of Storefront
Storefront’s features are focused on eCommerce, allowing you to quickly and easily set up shop with WooCommerce.
### Responsive
Storefront is fully responsive and adjusts to fit any size screen.
On mobile devices, a nice secondary menu is stickied to the bottom of the screen, allowing visitors to manage their accounts, search products, and checkout from anywhere.
**Related:** Best [Responsive Themes for WordPress](https://www.isitwp.com/best-responsive-wordpress-themes/).
### Full WooCommerce Integration
Storefront was designed by the developers of WooCommerce specifically to integrate with the popular eCommerce plugin.
As long as you have the WooCommerce plugin activated, a product search bar and shopping cart link will appear automatically in the header — no need to mess around with settings or code.
### ECommerce Homepage Template
Storefront includes a special template designed for your homepage. It displays product categories, recent and features products, sales, and top-rated products. (You can see it in action in the screenshot at the top of the page.)
To use it, create a new page and select the Homepage template.
Then navigate to Settings » Reading and under Front page displays at the top, select A static page. Then select the page you just created.
Make sure to click the **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the page. Now your homepage will display your product categories.
### Customization Options
Under Appearance » Customize, you can adjust your background color, header styles, link color, typography, button styles, and other settings to make your store your own.
Storefront has just enough options to give your site a different look, without being bloated with an overwhelming array of features like many other plugins.
### Widget Areas
Storefront includes 6 widget areas:
* **Below Header**: Displays full-width underneath the header. Good for sale banners and other CTAs
* **Sidebar**: Displays on every page and post, either on the right or left (you can change the side under Appearance » Customize » Layout)
* **Footer 1, 2, 3, and 4**: displays in four columns in the footer, on every page of your site
### Easily Extendable
WooCommerce and Storefront are a hugely popular combination with a large and supportive community. There are numerous child themes as well as free and premium plugins available if you want to add certain features or change the look of your shop.
Plugin extensions include:
* **Reviews**: allows you to highlight your best reviews on your homepage or across your site
* **Pricing tables**: display features of your products and services in an eye-catching way
* **Parallax hero**: add an attention grabbing large image to your homepage with parallax effects
There are also over a dozen official child themes available, most specializing in a different industry or niche. They include:
* **Galleria**: for fashion and design stores
* **Outlet**: for tech-related shops
* **Toyshop**: a fun theme for kids
* **Pharmacy**: for the health industry
* **Hotel**: designed for those selling services and booking time; also integrates with WooCommerce Bookings plugin
## Documentation and Support
WooCommerce has a dedicated section of their site that has documentation for all their themes and plugins, including Storefront. Their [Installation & Configuration](https://docs.woocommerce.com/document/installation-configuration/) document will walk you through installing and setting up the theme just like it is in the demo, and show you how the main features work.
Users of the free Storefront theme can post in the WordPress.org support forums if they need assistance. The community is very active there and most support requests are resolved fairly quickly.
If you purchase any premium child themes or plugins from WooCommerce, you get access to their Helpdesk where you can open a support ticket.

## Our Verdict on Storefront
Storefront is an excellent basic theme for eCommerce sites using the WooCommerce plugin. The integration is seamless and makes it easy to create a fully functional online shop.
The theme is very plain looking, but this is intentional. Its lightweight and bloat-free design make it easy to extend and customize to suit your individual needs.
Users who want a more unique look or need more advanced options for their online store will probably benefit from investing in an official WooCommerce child theme and/or plugins for Storefront.
