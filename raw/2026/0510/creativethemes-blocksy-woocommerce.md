## Conversion-Boosting Features

- **Smart Assets Loading** — Serves only the CSS and JavaScript required by active WooCommerce features, reducing page weight and boosting speed.
- **Product Filters** — Customizable category, attribute or price filters that appear inline or in an off-canvas drawer.
- **Accessibility Ready** — Semantic HTML, ARIA landmarks and keyboard navigation for WCAG compliant browsing.
- **Translation Ready** — Easily translate entire shop.

- **Variation Swatches** — Color, image or button swatches instead of dropdowns.
- **Product Wishlist** — Save favourite items, share on social media.
- **Quick View** — Modal with gallery, price and add-to-cart controls.
- **Advanced Reviews** — Photos, headings, user voting and summaries. Average score, voting system, informative title, image upload + lightbox.
- **Size Guide** — Size charts to reduce returns.
- **Compare Products** — Side-by-side comparison.
- **Product Brands** — Logos, descriptions, filter by brand.
- **Stock Scarcity** — Low-stock notices and progress bars.
- **Free Shipping Bar** — Progress bar tracking cart total against free-shipping threshold.
- **Product Custom Tabs** — Extra info tabs, display conditions per product/category.
- **Product Waitlist** — Waitlist form for out-of-stock products, email alerts.
- **Product Video** — YouTube, Vimeo or self-hosted clips in product gallery.
- **Sale Countdown Timer** — Countdown for scheduled sales.
- **Social Sharing** — Share buttons for products.
- **Smart Off-Canvas Mini Cart** — AJAX side cart, instant updates, cross-sell suggestions.
- **Payment Icons** — Display accepted payment options.
- **Optimized For Mobile Sales** — Responsive layouts, lazy assets, touch-friendly controls.
