## Overview

Impeccable is an open-source AI design skill by Paul Bakaus that enhances AI assistants (Claude Code, Cursor, OpenCode, etc.) to generate professional, non-generic frontend UIs, avoiding common "AI slop" patterns.

## Benchmarks
- Boosts UI quality by **59%** in benchmarks (dashboards, landing pages) without model changes
- Evaluated against gpt-5.4 and Qwen 3.6 Plus with 15 briefs
- Key insight: forcing models to name and reject their first 3 font/color instincts is the single biggest quality improvement

## Criticisms
- **Token overhead** — loading 7 reference files adds significant context per command
- **Opinionated tastes** — some users find the design preferences too specific
- **Rapid updates** — can be hard to keep up, but the team addresses issues quickly
