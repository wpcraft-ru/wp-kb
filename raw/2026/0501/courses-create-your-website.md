# Create your website on WordPress.com

In this beginner-friendly course, we will show you how to create a website on WordPress.com.

In this series of videos, we'll walk you through the essentials of getting your website online. You'll learn how to activate a theme, edit the design, and set up essential elements like your homepage, header, and footer.

You'll also discover how to customize your site's colors and fonts to match your vision and, finally, launch your site to the world. Join us for this hands-on, practical course and take the first step in establishing your online presence.

By following along with this course, you will learn how to:

- Find and activate a block theme.
- Build a homepage and add content using blocks and patterns.
- Create additional pages for your site.
- Customize your header and footer.
- Style your site for a cohesive look and feel.
- Edit templates for structural changes.
- Launch your site for the world to see.
