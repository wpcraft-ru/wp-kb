# Choose a WordPress host

Every WordPress site needs a host. This guide explains [WordPress.com hosting](https://wordpress.com/hosting/) and covers the most common questions you may have about hosting a website.

## What is WordPress.com Hosting?

"WordPress" (or WordPress.org) is a free software that can be installed with a number of companies. WordPress.com is one, and there are hundreds of others.

By purchasing a [WordPress.com plan](https://wordpress.com/plans/), you will have everything you need to create and host a website and share it with the world. Your site will be hosted on WordPress.com. WordPress.com plans are all-inclusive managed hosting, so you do not need to host your site elsewhere.

Read on to learn what's included in WordPress.com hosting, or visit our guide to learn how to [import your existing website from another host.](https://wordpress.com/support/import/import-an-entire-wordpress-site/)

## What is Managed Hosting?

"Managed hosting" means that we take care of all the technical aspects of hosting your site, like backups, security, caching, and more. If you choose to host your site with another host, you would typically be responsible for these functions. This is why WordPress.com is ideal for those who don't want to worry about the technical stuff, and just focus on creating and growing your website.

## Common Questions About WordPress.com Hosting

### Can I start with managed hosting on WordPress.com for free?

Yes. A free site on WordPress.com includes editing, themes, and a free .wordpress.com site address. It's a great option if you're just getting started with WordPress, and you can upgrade your plan for additional features at any time.

### Can I migrate my WordPress site from another host?

Yes. You can export content from your current site and bring that content into a WordPress.com site. With our paid plans, there are additional options for importing that include theme customizations and plugins. You can choose the best import option for your needs in our [Import support guide](https://wordpress.com/support/import/).

### Does managed hosting take care of updates?

Hosting at WordPress.com ensures your site will always be up-to-date with the [latest version of WordPress.](https://wordpress.com/support/check-your-wordpress-version/) We take care of your site's updates to the WordPress software as well as updating versions of PHP.

### Does managed hosting include security?

Absolutely! We take security very seriously and have a dedicated team whose job it is to ensure the safety of all websites on WordPress.com. [Here is a complete overview of what to expect.](https://wordpress.com/support/security/)

### Do you back up my website?

Yes, we automatically save a backup of your site each day (and often multiple times per day if you are making numerous large changes.) You can [restore a backup](https://wordpress.com/support/restore/) on our Business and Commerce plans. Free sites and sites on the Personal or Premium plans cannot directly access backups, but our systems behind the scenes ensure your content is kept safe and secure.

### Can I use my own domain?

Yes! If you already have a domain, you can [connect](https://wordpress.com/support/domains/connect-existing-domain/) or [transfer](https://wordpress.com/support/domains/incoming-domain-transfer/) it to WordPress.com. All our annual plans also include a free year of domain registration for new domains.

### Are SSL certificates included?

Yes. We issue [SSL certificates](https://wordpress.com/support/domains/https-ssl/) for all sites on WordPress.com and update them for domains that are hosted on our servers.

### What is Jetpack?

Jetpack is included in all websites for WordPress.com, with [Jetpack Professional included](https://wordpress.com/support/jetpack/jetpack-on-plugin-enabled-sites/) on our plugin-enabled plans. At lower plans, we still include [many of the features from Jetpack](https://wordpress.com/support/jetpack/) to enhance your site's features.

### Is email hosting included?

While not included in the cost of the plan, you can add on email hosting at great value. Learn more about [adding email hosting](https://wordpress.com/support/add-email/) to your site.

### Where is my site hosted?

WordPress.com hosts your site on thousands of servers in several data centers in different parts of the US and worldwide. You can view [a real-time traffic map of our data centers](https://automattic.com/automattic-data-centers/) showing their global distribution and [choose your site's primary data center](https://wordpress.com/support/choose-your-sites-primary-data-center/) for optimal performance.

## Plugin-Enabled Plans

This section applies to sites with our [WordPress.com Personal, Premium, Business, and Commerce plans](https://wordpress.com/pricing/). For free sites, [upgrade your plan](https://wordpress.com/support/manage-purchases/upgrade-your-plan/) to access this feature.

### Can I use a custom theme?

Yes! With a paid plan, you can [upload custom or third-party themes](https://wordpress.com/support/themes/uploading-setting-up-custom-themes/) as well as plugins.

### Can I use plugins or third-party themes?

With our plans, you can install any third-party tool on your site, including ecommerce plugins like WooCommerce, SEO plugins like Yoast Premium, and any of the [50,000+ other plugins](https://wordpress.org/plugins/) and [10,000+ themes](https://wordpress.org/themes/) available in the WordPress ecosystem. There are some plugins we don't support, usually because they conflict with identical features already included in our platform. Check the [list of Incompatible Plugins here.](https://wordpress.com/support/plugins/incompatible-plugins/)

### Is WordPress.com ideal for ecommerce?

Yes. With a paid plan, you can integrate plugins like [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.com/plugins/woocommerce/) to build a store to sell products and services online. We are the same company that develops and supports the WooCommerce plugin, so WordPress.com is the best home for your online store.

## Performance

### How fast is WordPress.com hosting?

WordPress.com offers 99.999% uptime and the fastest WP bench (measures how many WP queries per second) of any hosting company in any price tier. A website owner should never have to compromise on speed when choosing a host, so we optimize our platform to ensure a lightning-fast experience for your website's visitors.

### Does plan level affect loading time?

Plans don't make a difference in the site's loading time, as all WordPress.com sites are optimized for speed. The speed of your site will only be affected by the theme you are using, the size of your images, and your installed plugins. [You can learn more about speed optimization here.](https://wordpress.com/support/site-speed/)

Yes, we include [detailed stats](https://wordpress.com/support/stats/) plus the ability to connect to [Google Analytics](https://wordpress.com/support/google-analytics/).

Yes! We offer [varying levels of support](https://wordpress.com/support/help-support-options/) for all our paying customers, as well as public support forums for free users.

## Why Choose WordPress.com as Your Host?

There are a LOT of different companies in the WordPress ecosystem. If you're stressing about having a domain from one company, a theme from another, and hosting with yet another, you can greatly simplify your setup without compromising on capabilities or value on WordPress.com:

- **All-in-one solution:** Everything you need in one place — hosting, domains, plugins, and themes.
- **Seamless integration:** Everything works together seamlessly without compatibility issues.
- **Great value:** Bundling hosting, domain, and themes into one package means cost savings.
- **Expert support:** Comprehensive support for all aspects of your website.
- **Scalability:** No limits on traffic or bandwidth. Add new functionality as your site grows.

By choosing WordPress.com, you can have peace of mind knowing that your website is in good hands, and you can focus on what matters most: creating great content and engaging with your audience.
