# Choose a website or a blog

If you're creating a brand-new site, you might wonder if you need a website, a blog, or a website with a blog. This guide will show you how to create any of these options on WordPress.com.

## Website vs. Blog

There is very little that is different between a website and a blog. Both are created using the tools available on WordPress.com, which is both a hosting service and a website builder all in one. The main differences:

- **A website** focuses more on "static" pages. Static pages are pages of content that don't frequently change, like an About page or a Contact page.
- **A blog** has more dynamic content. Blog posts, or articles, are published by the blog owner regularly and can appear in social feeds and be emailed to subscribers.

You will follow the same steps to set up a website or a blog. The difference comes from which features in the site's dashboard that you use:

- If you **don't want a blog** on your website, you just won't use the blogging features – most notably the Posts section.
- If you want **only a blog or a website with a blog**, you will use the blogging features, including the Posts area, Subscribers, and more.

## Creating a Blog

A blog is a type of website that puts your latest articles front and center. If you intend to write new posts, publish photos regularly, or share news, then a blog is most likely the best fit for your goals. Visit [this signup form](https://wordpress.com/start) to get started on your blog.

For guidance, visit our quick guide to [set up your blog in five steps](https://wordpress.com/support/five-step-blog-setup/) or watch our beginner-friendly video course on [how to create a blog](https://wordpress.com/support/courses/create-your-blog/).

## Adding a Blog to an Existing Website

It's common for website owners to add a blogging section to their website. Publishing new content regularly on a blog is a great way to improve your website's search engine ranking, share news, and increase engagement.

You don't have to install anything on your website to add a blog. To add a blog to your website:

1. In your dashboard, navigate to Posts → Add New to write and publish your first post.
2. Create a page to display your blog posts. You can use an [automatically generated blog page](https://wordpress.com/support/posts-page/) or [create your own](https://wordpress.com/support/create-a-separate-page-for-blog-posts/).
3. Add your new posts page to your site's navigation menu.

You can also promote your posts on your site's homepage. To display posts on any page, add a [Blog Posts block](https://wordpress.com/support/wordpress-editor/blocks/blog-posts-block/) to draw attention to your latest updates.
