# Express Website Design Service

Let us build your custom 5-page website in 4 business days by answering a few questions and submitting your content.

> 14-day money-back guarantee

## Overview

Our website-building service is for anyone who wants a polished website fast: small businesses, personal websites, bloggers, clubs or organizations, and more. Answer a few questions, submit your content, and we'll handle the rest. You'll receive your customized 5-page site within 4 business days!

View the [pricing details for the service here](https://wordpress.com/website-design-service/#get-started). Additionally, the [Premium plan](https://wordpress.com/plans/) or higher is required, which offers fast, secure hosting, video embedding, 13 GB of storage, a free domain for one year, and expert support. If you opt to use an existing site with the plan, you'll only need to purchase the website design service.

Additional pages can be purchased during signup.

## AI Content & Images

Don't worry if you don't have images or content for every page. After checkout, you'll have an option to opt into AI text creation. Our design team can select images and use AI to create your site content, all of which you can edit later using the editor. If you select the blog page during sign up, we'll even create three blog posts for you to get you started!

## Ecommerce Store Option

We offer an ecommerce store setup option which includes setup of the WooCommerce shop landing page, cart, checkout, and my account pages, along with additional pages you choose while signing up. Please note, individual product setup, payments, taxes, shipping, and other WooCommerce extensions or settings are not included. A [Business plan](https://wordpress.com/plans/) purchase is required for a store site.

## Process

After you check out, you'll fill out a content upload form that includes any design preferences and reference sites. While we can't guarantee an exact match, we'll consider all your feedback during site construction. Once we've looked over your content, we'll drop you an email. If everything looks good, we'll let you know when to expect your shiny new site. If we need to chat about [the guidelines](https://wordpress.com/support/website-design-service/#content-guidelines), we'll reach out. You'll receive an email when your new site is ready — always within four business days.

## Post-Build

While this service does not include revisions, once you've received your completed site, you can modify everything using the WordPress editor – colors, text, images, adding new pages, and anything else you'd like to tweak. Furthermore, our Premium plan offers expert support if you need assistance.

If you choose to use your current WordPress.com site, we will set your existing pages to draft mode; you can publish them later as desired. Any existing posts will remain untouched. We'll create new pages with your provided content while applying a new, customized theme. However, we won't edit any existing content on your site's pages.

Yes, our support team will help you connect your existing domain name to your site after the build is complete.

## Content Guidelines

To ensure a smooth process, please keep these friendly reminders in mind:

- **Fresh Content Only:** Please provide original content rather than requesting migrations or content from existing pages.
- **Timely Submission:** Submit your content within 14 days of purchase to keep things on track. If we don't receive it in time, we'll use AI-generated text and stock images.
- **Bite-sized Content:** Keep each page under 5,000 characters. Longer content will be trimmed using AI.
- **Design Approach:** We follow established design guidelines while customizing your site to reflect your brand.
- **Stay Connected:** After you submit your content, we'll review it. If everything meets our guidelines, we'll notify you when your site is ready.
- **Perfect As-Is:** We aim to get it right the first time! While revisions aren't included, you can always make updates later.
- **Plugin Potential:** Add options like appointments, courses, property listings, memberships, payments, animations, and more after we finish your site.
- **Custom Requests:** For fully custom solutions or pixel-perfect recreations, we can connect you with an [expert WordPress agency partner](https://wordpress.com/website-design-service/get-started?ref=supportpage), with projects starting at $5,000.
