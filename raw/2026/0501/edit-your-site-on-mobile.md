# Edit your site on mobile or tablet

Use the [free Jetpack app](https://apps.wordpress.com/mobile/) to manage and edit your WordPress.com site on your phone or tablet. This guide will show you how to install the app, log in, and start publishing and editing content from your mobile device.

## Install and Log In

To manage your WordPress.com website from your phone or tablet:

1. Visit [wp.com/app](https://wp.com/app) from your mobile device or search for the Jetpack mobile app in [Google Play (Android)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.jetpack.android) or the [App Store (Apple)](https://apps.apple.com/app/jetpack-for-wordpress/id1565481562).
2. Install the app.
3. Open the app and log in using your WordPress.com account information.

## Navigation

The Jetpack app has a bottom navigation bar with these key areas:

- **My Site:** Manage posts, pages, media, subscribers, stats, settings, and more.
- **Reader:** Explore and follow other sites in the WordPress.com Reader.
- **Notifications:** See likes, comments, and other activity.
- **Me:** Manage your account profile, app settings, support, and more.

## Switch Between Sites

If your WordPress.com account is connected to more than one site:

1. Tap "My Site" in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Tap the downward-facing arrow next to the site name at the top of the screen.
3. A list of your connected sites will appear.
4. Tap the site you want to manage.

If you cannot see your site, confirm that you're logged in with the correct WordPress.com account.

## Create New Posts and Pages

1. Tap "My Site" in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Select either Posts or Pages.
3. Tap the + icon to start a new post or page.
4. Enter a title.
5. Start writing text or tap the + button to add images and other blocks.
6. When ready, tap Publish in the top-right corner or tap the back button to save as draft.

> **Tip:** If your mobile internet connection is unstable, save drafts frequently by tapping the ⋮ icon in the upper right corner and then the Save option.

## Edit Existing Content

1. Tap "My Site" in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Select either Posts or Pages.
3. Tap on the page or post you wish to edit.

### Editing Homepage on Block Themes
If your site uses a block theme:
1. Select Homepage.
2. Select Templates.
3. Choose your site's homepage template (may be called Home, Blog Home, Front Page, or Index).
4. Edit the content as needed.
5. Tap Update or Save.

## Add Images and Media

1. Tap "My Site" → Media (iPhone) or More → Media (Android).
2. Tap the + icon and choose from:
   - Take a new photo or video using your device's camera.
   - Choose an existing file from your device.
   - Search the [free photo library](https://wordpress.com/support/finding-free-images-and-other-media/pexels/) for royalty-free images.
   - Search for a GIF.

You can also upload media while editing a page or post by tapping the + button and choosing Image, Gallery, Video, or another media block.

### Troubleshooting Media Uploads
- Check that your device has a stable internet connection.
- Try uploading a smaller file or compressing large images.
- Verify that your site has enough available storage space.

## Reorder Blocks

### Move One Position at a Time
1. Tap the block you want to move — the block toolbar appears.
2. Scroll right on the toolbar until up/down arrow buttons appear.
3. Tap the up arrow to move the block higher, or down arrow to move it lower.
4. Tap Update or Save.

### Drag and Drop
1. Tap and hold the block you want to move until it lifts off the page.
2. Drag the block up or down to its new position.
3. Release your finger to drop the block in place.
4. Tap Update or Save.

## Access Site Settings

1. Tap "My Site" in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Select More and then "Site Settings".
3. From here, update details like site title, tagline, privacy settings, and more.

For questions or issues with the Jetpack mobile app, visit the [mobile app help page](https://apps.wordpress.com/support/mobile/).
