# Schedule a call with a WordPress.com expert

WordPress.com Business and Commerce plans include access to free 1:1 onboarding sessions. These 15-minute video calls connect you with an Onboarding Specialist who can help you get started, answer your early questions, and guide you through setting up your site. It's personalized support designed to help you launch with confidence.

Onboarding sessions are a great fit if you:

- Recently purchased a plan and would like a little help getting started.
- Need help navigating all the options and settings.
- Want guidance before diving into all that WordPress.com has to offer.
- Learn better with live, personal, one-on-one help from a WordPress.com expert.
- Have early questions about building your site or what to do post-launch.

## Eligibility

Onboarding sessions are available if you:

- Have a Business or Commerce plan
- Can attend a session in English
- Are within your first few weeks of creating your site

On a different plan? No problem — contact our support team anytime.

## How It Works

Each session is a free 15-minute video call with a WordPress.com Onboarding Specialist.

- You share your screen (or we share ours) over a video call.
- We learn about your goals. What are you building? What do you need help with?
- We guide you through next steps — setup, settings, tools, domains, or whatever you need.
- You leave with a clear plan for what's next, plus a follow-up email with notes and resources. You can also request a recording of your session.

## What Can Be Covered

- Dashboard orientation
- Basic site setup
- Connecting a custom domain name
- Getting ready to launch your site
- Starting a migration or kicking off an Express Website Design Service site build
- And more — bring your questions!

## Duration

15 minutes. It's enough time to answer your questions, show you around, and give you a clear path forward without taking over your day.

## How to Schedule

If you recently purchased a Business or Commerce plan, check your inbox. You should receive an email inviting you to schedule a session shortly after your purchase.

Didn't receive the email? Contact support, and we'll help you get scheduled.

## What to Prepare

Bring your questions! It helps to be logged into your WordPress.com account so we can look at your site together. You'll need a device with a camera and microphone for the video call.

## After the Session

We'll make sure you leave with clear next steps and a solid plan for moving forward. If you have additional questions later, our support team is always here to help.

## FAQ

### Can I reschedule or cancel?
Yes. Use the link in your confirmation email to reschedule or cancel anytime.

### What plans include this?
Onboarding sessions are included with Business and Commerce plans. Ready to unlock this benefit? Upgrade your plan anytime.

### Are sessions available in other languages?
Not yet. Sessions are currently available in English only. For support in other languages, contact our support team.

We're here to help, whether or not you book a session.
